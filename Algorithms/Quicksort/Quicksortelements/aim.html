Aim

The aim of this virtual lab is to understand the concept and implementation of the Quick Sort algorithm.

Estimated Time

1 hour

Learning Objectives of the Experiment

  • Learn about the Quick Sort technique and its efficiency in sorting datasets.
  • Understand the divide-and-conquer approach used in Quick Sort.
  • Implement the Quick Sort algorithm through interactive animations.
  • Analyze the time complexity of Quick Sort in different scenarios.
  • Compare Quick Sort with other sorting algorithms to understand its advantages and disadvantages.
